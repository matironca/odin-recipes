Smoked Cabbagge

Description

Smoked cabbage is hands-off and a perfect side dish for whatever floats your boat. While you've got that smoker fired up, quarter a head of cabbage and slap it on in that last bit of cooking time. It pairs well with beef, pork, and chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 small head green cabbage
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 teaspoon steak seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup bacon bits

Steps

  1. Preheat a smoker to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C) according to manufacturer's directions. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.

  2. Place cabbage wedges on prepared baking sheet.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk olive oil, steak seasoning, salt, and pepper until evenly combined. Brush mixture over the cabbage wedges.

  4. Place baking sheet on the grate of the preheated smoker and smoke for 1 1/2 hours. Transfer to a serving dish and top with bacon pieces. Serve immediately.
